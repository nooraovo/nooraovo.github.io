Memory of Arashiyama

Arashiyama is a stunningly beautiful district on the outskirts of Kyoto, Japan where flower pedals cover the area like snow on a mountaintop. Arashiyama is Sodoi's singular coffee, crafted to bring out an amazing floral perfume essence in its aroma under a slightly lighter roast to preserve every nuance of fragrance.
